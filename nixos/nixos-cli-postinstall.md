Things to do before rebooting to NixOS
======================================

1. `/etc/nixos/configuration.nix`
2. disable root in `/etc/passwd`
3. `vim` ➡️ `$VIMRUNTIME` ➡️ `.vimrc` ➡️ `set mouse=`
4. HP 840 G2 BIOS > UEFI > Boot > Custom > `\EFI\BOOT\BOOTX64.EFI`
5. reboot
6. [remove old gens](https://discourse.nixos.org/t/list-and-delete-nixos-generations/29637/8)
